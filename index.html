About Me

I grew up in the slums of DC. Always observing things closely and being fascinated by the potential of creativity, I used creativity as my escape; whether it was drawing, producing my own music to listen to, or simply thinking of the most bizarre ways to fix everyday problems around me. Today, I use it to create webpages (like this one!) and complete UX projects for myself and others.
